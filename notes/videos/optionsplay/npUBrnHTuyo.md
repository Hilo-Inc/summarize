## OptionsPlay + StockCharts: Options Trading Workflow

**Presenter:** Tony Zhang, Chief Strategist at OptionsPlay

---

### The Core Problem

Traditional options workflow wastes time: research stock → pull up options chain → discover trade doesn't work → start over. ~50% of options research gets abandoned this way before a valid trade is found.

### The Flipped Workflow

**Pre-qualify trades by options quality first, then only research the winners.**

1. Build a stock scan in StockCharts (by sector, market cap, technical indicators)
2. Load that scan into OptionsPlay Strategy Center
3. OptionsPlay ranks every stock by risk-reward for your chosen strategy
4. Only research the top 3–5 stocks — everything else is eliminated before you look at a chart

### What Gets Pre-Validated Automatically

- Risk/reward ratio
- Liquidity & open interest
- Earnings dates proximity
- Premium levels (too cheap or too expensive for your strategy)
